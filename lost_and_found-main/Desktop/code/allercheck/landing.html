Nutritious Eating with Allergies

Balanced nutrition while avoiding allergens

Building a Balanced Diet

Managing food allergies doesn't mean sacrificing nutrition. With careful planning, you can maintain a healthy, balanced diet while avoiding allergens.

  • Focus on whole foods like fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins
  • Read labels carefully to avoid hidden allergens
  • Consider consulting with a registered dietitian
  • Explore allergen-free alternatives

Common Nutrient Deficiencies

People with food allergies may be at risk for certain nutrient deficiencies:

Calcium/Vitamin D From dairy restrictions
Protein/Iron From egg or meat restrictions
B Vitamins From grain restrictions
Vitamin E From nut restrictions

About allercheck

allercheck was created to help people with food allergies live safer, healthier lives. Our mission is to provide reliable, easy-to-use tools that empower individuals to make informed decisions about their food choices.
